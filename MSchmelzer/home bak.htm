Welcome to...

Winona Pentecostal Church

At Winona Pentecostal Church, our goal is to create an evironment that encourages a Growing relationship with Jesus Christ, to Serve the needs of people in our community, and Reaching out with the gospel of Jesus Christ.

Jesus spent His last days on the earth opening His disciple's understanding of the scriptures and commanding that they spread the wonderful news of salvation to every creature. We believe that there is no greater message for the world today, and consider it one of the greatest obligations that the church must fulfill.
